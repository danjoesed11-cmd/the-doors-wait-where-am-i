What Awaits

Every Door is a Gamble

🚪
Three Doors Per Round
Each round presents three mystery doors. Behind each: combat, traps, loot, companions, death, or glory. You can never be sure which.
⚔️
Weapons & Arsenal
Find weapons across seven rarities. Carry three at a time. When your arsenal is full, choose what to drop and what to keep.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Companions & Bonds
Recruit fighters, healers, mages, and scouts. Relationships grow as you survive together — and break when things go wrong.
🎲
Dice-Based Combat
Combat plays out round by round. Your weapon damage, luck, combat wins, and companions all tip the scales in your favour.
💾
Three Save Files
Three separate runs. Each door you survive through is auto-saved — weapons, companions, and all. Come back whenever.
📈
Escalating Fate
The deeper you go, the darker the corridor. Round 15 is nothing like round 1. Win conditions only appear when you've earned them.

Getting Started

How to Play

01
Pick a Save File
Choose one of three save files to begin your run — or continue where you left off.
02
Choose a Door
Three doors appear each round. Tap one. There is no safe choice — only your best guess.
03
Face What's Behind It
Combat, traps, boons, weapons, companions, lore — or instant death. React accordingly.
04
Survive Long Enough
Win conditions appear from round 10 onward. Defeat Satan, ascend as an angel, or claim the throne.
